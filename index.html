ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Mike, a student at Northeastern University. I’ve always liked how audio could build narratives, whether it’s through film, TV, and especially games! Sound design has always been a passion of mine, and it’s because I want people to ask the same things I asked when I started: “That sounded awesome! How’d they do that?” I mainly come from a film and television background, where I specialize in recording foley, field recordings, and working audio in live broadcasts, but I know I want to expand my experience and skills into something in the gaming industry. Sound design, with its focus on immersion and narrative, seems to be the natural fit for me.
